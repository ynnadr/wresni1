About Us

Wresni Real Estate & Villa Management is a boutique property company based in the heart of Bali. Founded in honor of my beloved mother, Wresni, whose name stands for honesty, hard work, and genuine kindness, our company was created to continue her legacy through heartfelt, trustworthy service.

With over 20 years of hands-on experience in Bali’s real estate and hospitality industries—including international exposure working in Hong Kong—I have built Wresni as a reflection of both professional excellence and personal values. Our mission is simple: to guide, protect, and support every client as if they were family.

At Wresni, we do more than manage properties — we manage dreams, investments, and peace of mind.
